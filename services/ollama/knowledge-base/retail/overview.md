# Retail Support Overview

## What this line helps with

- Any online shopping question: orders, tracking, shipping, delivery, returns, refunds, exchanges, products, sizes, promos, and pickup.
- Help resolving order problems, payment issues, and damaged or wrong items.
- Guidance for situations not in our order FAQ using standard retail support steps.

## How to ask

- Have your demo order number ready if you have one (starts with ORD-).
- Say whether the item was shipped, delivered, or picked up in store.

## Questions not in our lists

- See guide.md for general troubleshooting: delay, wrong item, partial shipment, promo not applied.
