# Retail Orders

## Order tracking

- Demo order numbers start with ORD- plus 8 digits.
- Tracking updates within 4 hours after warehouse scan.
- Standard shipping 3 to 5 business days; express 1 to 2 days.

## Shipping delays

- Weather delays show on tracking automatically.
- No movement for 5 days: open delay inquiry online.

## Order changes

- Address change until status shows packed.
- Item swaps not supported after payment; cancel and reorder.

## Store pickup

- Ready email when status is ready for pickup.
- Hold 7 days; uncollected orders refunded minus 5 dollar restocking fee.

## Payment issues

- Failed payments retry once after 24 hours.
- Duplicate charges reversed in 3 to 5 business days demo mode.

## Partial shipments

- Remaining items ship separately; each has tracking link.
- Shipping charged once per order unless split at warehouse.

## Gift orders

- Gift message on packing slip; prices hidden on gift receipt option.

## Help for order questions not listed

- Use guide.md steps; escalate with order number to support form in demo.
