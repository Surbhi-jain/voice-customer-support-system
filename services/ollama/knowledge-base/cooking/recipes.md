# Cooking Recipes

## Pav bhaji

- Boil 3 medium potatoes and 1 cup peas until soft; mash coarsely.
- Sauté 1 onion, 2 tomatoes, and pav bhaji masala for 10 minutes.
- Combine with mash; cook 15 minutes on medium heat.
- Serve hot with buttered pav rolls, chopped onion, and lemon.

## Idli sambar

- Soak 2 cups rice and half cup urad dal 4 hours; grind; ferment overnight until bubbly.
- Steam idlis in greased molds 10 to 12 minutes until spongy.
- Sambar: pressure-cook toor dal; sauté onion, tomato, sambar powder, tamarind, and vegetables; simmer 20 minutes.
- Serve hot idlis with sambar and chutney.

## Aloo paratha

- Knead whole wheat dough; rest 20 minutes.
- Boil and mash 3 potatoes with salt, chili, cumin, and coriander for stuffing.
- Roll dough, add stuffing, seal, roll gently; cook on hot tawa with ghee until golden both sides.
- Serve with yogurt and pickle.

## Dosa

- Use fermented batter (rice and urad dal), spread thin on hot pan with oil.
- Cook until crisp and golden; serve with sambar and chutney.

## Chole bhature

- Soak and pressure-cook chickpeas; cook with onion, tomato, chole masala, and ginger-garlic.
- Bhature: knead refined flour dough with yogurt; rest; roll and deep-fry until puffed and golden.
- Serve hot chole with bhature, onion, and lemon.

## Paneer butter masala

- Sauté onion and tomato; add cashew paste, spices, and water; simmer.
- Add paneer cubes; finish with butter and cream; serve with naan or rice.

## Dal makhani

- Slow-cook black lentils and kidney beans with tomato, butter, cream, and garam masala until thick.
- Finish with kasuri methi; serve with rice or roti.

## Rajma chawal

- Soak rajma; pressure-cook; simmer with onion, tomato, and rajma masala until thick.
- Serve over steamed basmati rice with salad.

## Upma

- Roast semolina lightly; sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, and vegetables.
- Add water, salt, and semolina; stir until fluffy; garnish with coriander and lemon.

## Poha

- Rinse thick poha; drain. Sauté onion, potato, peanuts, turmeric, and green chili.
- Add poha, salt, and lemon; cook 5 minutes; garnish with coriander.

## Maggi noodles

- Boil 2 cups water; add Maggi masala and noodles; cook 2 minutes stirring.
- Optional: add peas, carrot, or egg; serve hot.

## Tomato basil pasta

- Cook spaghetti al dente. Simmer crushed tomatoes, garlic, and basil 20 minutes.
- Toss pasta with sauce and olive oil.

## Masala omelette

- Whisk eggs with salt, turmeric, and chili. Fry onion and green chili; add eggs; cook through.

## Vegetable pulao

- Soak basmati 20 minutes. Fry cumin, bay leaf, and mixed vegetables; add rice and water.
- Cook covered on low 18 minutes; rest and fluff.

## Chicken curry

- Brown chicken; sauté onion, ginger-garlic, tomato, and curry spices.
- Add water; simmer until chicken is cooked through.

## Biryani

- Par-cook basmati. Layer with fried onions, marinated meat or vegetables, mint, and saffron milk.
- Cook sealed on low heat 25 minutes; rest before serving.

## Fried rice

- Use day-old rice. High heat stir-fry vegetables, garlic, rice, and soy sauce; add scrambled egg.

## Pancakes

- Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, egg, milk, and melted butter. Cook on griddle until golden.

## Chocolate cake

- Cream butter and sugar; beat in eggs. Fold flour, cocoa, baking powder, and milk.
- Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 to 35 minutes.

## Grilled cheese sandwich

- Butter bread; grill with cheese until melted and golden on both sides.

## Smoothie bowl

- Blend frozen banana, berries, and milk. Top with granola and fresh fruit.
