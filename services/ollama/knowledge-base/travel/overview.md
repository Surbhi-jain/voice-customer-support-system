# Travel Support Overview

## What this line helps with

- Any travel and airline question: flights, bookings, changes, delays, cancellations, seats, baggage, airports, documents, and trip planning.
- Help before, during, and after your trip in demo mode.
- Guidance for issues not in our flight FAQ using standard airline support steps.

## How to ask

- Share flight date, route, or booking reference if you have one.
- Say whether issue is before travel, at airport, or after landing.

## Questions not listed in our files

- See guide.md for delays, missed connections, seat changes, and document checks.
