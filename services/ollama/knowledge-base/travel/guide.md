# Travel Support Guide

## General help for any trip issue

- Confirm booking on airline app or email confirmation.
- For delays over 3 hours, meal vouchers may apply at participating airports.
- Rebooking options depend on fare type: flex vs basic.

## Before you fly

- Check passport validity 6 months beyond return for international demo routes.
- Arrive domestic 2 hours early; international 3 hours.
- Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure.

## At the airport

- Baggage drop closes 45 minutes before domestic departure in demo policy.
- Gate changes announced on boards and app notifications.

## After travel

- Lost baggage: report before leaving airport; trace within 24 hours.
- Complaints: reference record locator and flight number.

## Help when your question is not listed

- Match to flights.md or baggage.md topic.
- Urgent day-of-travel: advise airline counter or call number on ticket.
