# Travel Baggage

## Carry-on allowance

- One bag 22 x 14 x 9 inches plus one personal item.
- Basic fare boards last; bin space first-come.

## Checked baggage

- First bag 35 dollars domestic economy demo.
- Second bag 45 dollars; 50 pound limit per bag.

## Oversized and special items

- Sports equipment counts as one checked bag at standard fee.
- Over 62 linear inches: extra 100 dollar handling fee.

## Lost baggage

- Report at baggage office before leaving airport.
- Trace reference within 24 hours; delivery within 48 hours when found.

## Prohibited items

- Lithium over 100 watt hours not in checked luggage.
- Liquids carry-on 3.4 ounces in quart bag.

## Excess weight

- Over 50 pounds: overweight fee 75 dollars per bag demo.
- Repack at counter or pay fee before accept.

## Strollers and car seats

- Gate-check free; does not count toward personal item on demo airline.

## Help for baggage questions not listed

- See guide.md; keep baggage tag number for lost bag trace.
