# Hotel Policies

## Cancellation policy

- Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival on standard rates.
- Non-refundable bookings cannot cancel; date changes may cost 50 dollars.
- Groups of 10 plus rooms need 7 days notice to cancel without penalty.

## Pet policy

- Dogs under 40 pounds in pet-friendly rooms on floor 4.
- Pet fee 35 dollars per night; max two pets; service animals free.

## Smoking policy

- Non-smoking indoors; 250 dollar cleaning fee for violations.
- Outdoor smoking area behind main entrance.

## Billing disputes

- Folios emailed within 24 hours of checkout.
- Disputes within 14 days via billing desk extension 4500.

## Incidentals and deposits

- Incidental hold 100 dollars per night on card at check-in.
- Released within 5 business days after checkout if no charges.

## Accessibility

- Accessible rooms on floor 1; request at booking.
- Wheelchair route via main lobby elevator to all public floors.

## General policy help

- For any policy not listed, apply the closest section and advise contacting front desk for exceptions in demo mode.
