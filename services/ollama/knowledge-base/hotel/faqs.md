# Hotel FAQs

## Check-in and check-out times

- Standard check-in begins at 3:00 PM local time.
- Express check-in from 1:00 PM for loyalty members.
- Check-out by 11:00 AM; late checkout until 2:00 PM costs 50 dollars unless pre-approved.

## Room upgrades and types

- Upgrades depend on day-of-arrival availability.
- Request at front desk or in-room phone menu option 2.
- Suite supplement typically 75 dollars per night in demo rates.

## Wi-Fi and amenities

- Wi-Fi password on key card sleeve: welcome2024.
- Fitness center 6 AM to 10 PM, second floor.
- Pool towels at pool desk; no reservation required.

## Housekeeping

- Daily housekeeping for stays of two nights or more.
- Turndown service 6 PM to 9 PM on request.
- Extra pillows and blankets within 20 minutes.

## Parking

- Self-parking 25 dollars per night; valet 40 dollars.
- EV charging west garage slots 12 through 15.

## Early check-in and late arrival

- Bag storage free if room not ready.
- After-hours check-in at 24-hour front desk; late arrival holds are honored until 6 AM.

## Room service and dining

- Room service menu in-room 6 AM to 11 PM.
- Breakfast buffet main restaurant 7 AM to 10:30 AM included on select rates.
