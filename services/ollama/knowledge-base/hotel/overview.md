# Hotel Support Overview

## What this line helps with

- Any guest question about staying at our demo hotel: bookings, check-in, check-out, rooms, amenities, housekeeping, billing, and policies.
- Help with reservations, changes, special requests, and on-stay problems.
- Guidance when your specific situation is not listed in our FAQ — we use standard hotel policies and guest care steps.

## How to ask

- Describe your stay dates, room type, or issue clearly.
- Ask follow-ups about fees, timing, or who to contact at the front desk.

## Questions not in the FAQ list

- We apply general hotel guest care: confirm reservation, explain policy, offer next step (front desk, housekeeping, or billing).
- See guide.md for late checkout, complaints, amenities, and billing disputes.
