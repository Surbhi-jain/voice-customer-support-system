# Banking Accounts (Demo)

## Opening an account

- Government ID and proof of address within 90 days required demo.
- Minimum opening deposit 25 dollars standard checking.

## Account fees

- Maintenance 12 dollars monthly; waived with 500 dollar average balance.
- Overdraft protection transfer fee 10 dollars per day used.

## Transfers

- Internal instant between demo accounts.
- External ACH 1 to 3 business days; 10,000 dollar daily limit demo.

## Statements

- E-statements free; paper 3 dollars per month.
- Available 5th business day each month.

## Closing an account

- Zero balance required; pending transactions must clear.
- Closure processed in 2 business days demo.

## Savings and interest

- Demo savings APY shown in app rate sheet; compounds monthly.
- No minimum balance on demo savings tier one.

## Direct deposit

- Routing and account number in app profile demo tab.
- Set up form PDF downloadable from demo site.

## Help for account questions not listed

- See guide.md; branch hours demo 9 AM to 5 PM weekdays.
