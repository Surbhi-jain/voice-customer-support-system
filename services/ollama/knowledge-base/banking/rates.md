# Interest and Policy Rates (Demo)

## Repo rate

- The repo rate is the rate at which the central bank lends short-term money to commercial banks.
- When the repo rate goes up, banks often raise lending rates; when it falls, borrowing can become cheaper over time.
- This demo does not show live central bank numbers; check official central bank announcements for the current repo rate.

## Reverse repo rate

- The rate at which the central bank absorbs excess liquidity from banks overnight.
- Usually sits slightly below the repo rate in normal policy setups.

## Base rate / benchmark rate

- Many banks price home loans and some business loans off a published base rate or benchmark plus a spread.
- Your exact loan rate in this demo is shown in the app rate sheet after account opening.

## Savings and deposit rates

- Demo savings APY is listed in the mobile app under Accounts then Rate sheet.
- Fixed deposits in demo: terms from 7 days to 5 years; rates vary by term and are not guaranteed until booked.

## Loan interest (demo)

- Personal loan demo range shown at application; final rate depends on credit profile in demo scoring.
- Credit card purchase APR and cash advance APR are in the card agreement PDF in the app.

## EMI and rate changes

- If you have a floating-rate demo loan, EMI may change when the benchmark rate changes after notice in the app.
- Fixed-rate demo loans keep the same rate for the agreed term unless you refinance.

## Fees vs interest

- Overdraft and late fees are flat charges in accounts.md; they are not the same as the repo rate or your loan interest rate.
