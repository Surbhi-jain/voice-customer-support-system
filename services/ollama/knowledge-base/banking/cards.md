# Banking Cards (Demo)

## Debit card replacement

- Report lost cards immediately; replacement 5 to 7 business days.
- Temporary digital card in mobile app within 1 hour demo.

## Credit limit increases

- Demo requires 6 months on-time payments.
- Request online; decision in 2 business days.

## Disputed charges

- Dispute within 60 days of statement date.
- Provisional credit may apply within 10 business days investigating.

## PIN reset

- Branch with photo ID or phone banking after verification.
- ATM PIN change 24 hours through phone demo line.

## International use

- Notify travel dates in app to reduce declines demo.
- Foreign transaction fee 2 percent demo credit cards.

## Card activation

- Activate new card at ATM or app before first use.
- Chip and PIN required demo debit at merchants.

## Autopay and payments

- Autopay from checking on due date in app.
- Minimum payment 25 dollars or balance due if lower demo.

## Help for card questions not listed

- See guide.md; lock card in app if stolen demo scenario.
