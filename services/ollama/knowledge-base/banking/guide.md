# Banking Demo Guide

## General help for any banking question

- Remind caller this is demo mode; no real money movement.
- For account access: verify identity steps in demo policy before discussing balances.
- Escalate suspected fraud to fraud hotline on back of demo card.

## Accounts and transfers

- Internal transfers instant between demo accounts.
- External ACH 1 to 3 business days; daily limits apply.
- Wire transfers not available in demo tier.

## Cards and PIN

- Lost card: lock in app immediately; replacement 5 to 7 days.
- PIN reset at branch with ID or phone banking after verification.

## Loans and fees

- Quote demo rates only from published schedule; no personalized advice.
- Maintenance fee waiver rules on checking with minimum balance.

## Disputes

- Card disputes within 60 days of statement; provisional credit possible in 10 days demo.

## Help when your question is not listed

- Map to accounts.md or cards.md; advise branch visit for complex demo scenarios.
