# Transfers and Payments (Demo)

## UPI

- Link bank account in demo app; set UPI PIN once.
- Daily limit demo 100,000 rupees; per transaction limit 50,000 rupees demo.
- Disputes: raise in app within 24 hours for wrong beneficiary demo flow.

## NEFT

- National Electronic Funds Transfer; batch settlements in hourly slots on business days.
- Demo cutoff times shown in app; credits typically same day or next business day.

## RTGS

- Real Time Gross Settlement for high-value transfers demo minimum 200,000 rupees.
- Processed in real time during RBI hours demo.

## IMPS

- Immediate Payment Service; 24 by 7 including holidays demo.
- Demo per-transaction cap 500,000 rupees.

## Internal transfers

- Between your own demo accounts: instant, no fee.

## International wire (demo)

- SWIFT code required; fees and FX rate quoted before confirm in demo app.
- Processing 2 to 5 business days demo.
