# Loans (Demo)

## Personal loan

- Demo eligibility: age 21 to 60, minimum income shown in app calculator.
- Documents: ID, address proof, last 3 months salary slips demo.
- Disbursal demo 2 to 3 business days after approval.

## Home loan

- Demo LTV up to 80 percent; property valuation required.
- Floating or fixed rate options; see rates.md for benchmark link.

## Car loan

- Demo tenure 1 to 7 years; vehicle hypothecation until closure.

## EMI

- Equated monthly installment includes principal and interest.
- Prepayment allowed demo with no penalty after 12 EMIs paid.

## Credit score (demo)

- Soft check at application; hard pull on final approval demo.
- Improve score: pay EMIs on time, keep credit utilization low demo tips.
